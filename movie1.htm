About Spiderman Movie

Spiderman is a popular superhero movie that follows the adventures of Peter Parker, a young man with spider-like abilities who uses his powers to fight crime and protect the city

The movie is known for its engaging storyline, dynamic characters, and impressive visual effects, making it a favorite among comic book and movie enthusiasts

Characters

  • Peter Parker
  • Mary Jane Watson
  • Green Goblin
